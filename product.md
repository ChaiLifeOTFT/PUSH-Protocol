# The P.U.S.H. Protocol
## A Framework for Sovereign Execution

### What is P.U.S.H.?

**P**urpose — Clarity of intent. Why are you building?
**U**nderstanding — Market, self, timing. What do you know?
**S**trategy — The plan. How will you execute?
**H**ustle — The work. When will you ship?

## The Protocol

### 1. PURPOSE (The Anchor)

Before any action, define:
- What problem are you solving?
- For whom?
- Why you?

*Exercise: Write your purpose statement in 50 words or less.*

### 2. UNDERSTANDING (The Map)

Research phase:
- Market landscape
- Competitor analysis
- Your unique advantage
- Timing and trends

*Tool: Use the REPLICANT Substrate Explorer to scan for opportunities.*

### 3. STRATEGY (The Plan)

Build the roadmap:
- 30-day sprint
- 90-day milestones
- Resource allocation
- Risk mitigation

*Framework: 5Ws1H (Who, What, When, Where, Why, How)*

### 4. HUSTLE (The Execution)

Do the work:
- Daily sprints
- Fibonacci cycles (1, 2, 3, 5, 8 hours)
- Measure progress
- Adapt and iterate

*Rule: No zero days. Something ships every 24 hours.*
